## 1. APPOINTMENT

The Company hereby appoints the Representative as an independent sales representative to promote and sell the Company's translation services. This appointment is non-exclusive.

## 2. TERM

This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for a period of **twelve (12) months**, unless terminated earlier in accordance with Section 10. The Agreement will automatically renew for successive one-year periods unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least 30 days prior to the end of the current term.

## 3. COMMISSION STRUCTURE

### 3.1 Commission Rate
The Representative shall earn a commission of **twenty percent (20%)** of the net revenue from each sale generated through clients referred by the Representative.

### 3.2 Qualified Sales
A "Qualified Sale" is defined as:
- A new client who was not previously registered in the Company's system
- The client must complete at least one paid transaction
- The sale must be trackable to the Representative's unique referral code or link

### 3.3 Commission Tracking Period
The Representative shall receive commission on all sales made by referred clients for a period of **ninety (90) days** from the date of the client's first purchase ("Cookie Period").

### 3.4 Commission Calculation
Commissions are calculated based on:
- Gross sale amount
- MINUS: Refunds, chargebacks, discounts, taxes, and shipping fees
- EQUALS: Net revenue (commission base)

**Example:**
- Client order: $500
- Discount applied: -$50
- Net revenue: $450
- Commission (20%): $90

## 4. PAYMENT TERMS

### 4.1 Payment Schedule
Commissions shall be calculated and paid on a **monthly basis**. The commission period runs from the 1st to the last day of each calendar month.

### 4.2 Payment Date
Payments shall be processed within **fifteen (15) business days** following the end of each commission period.

### 4.3 Minimum Payout
The minimum payout threshold is **$50.00 USD**. If the Representative's earned commissions do not meet this threshold, the balance will roll over to the following month.

## 5. REPRESENTATIVE'S RESPONSIBILITIES

The Representative agrees to:

5.1. Promote the Company's services in a professional and ethical manner

5.2. Provide accurate information about the Company's services and pricing

5.3. Not make false, misleading, or unauthorized claims about the Company

5.4. Comply with all applicable laws and regulations, including FTC disclosure requirements

5.5. Maintain confidentiality of client information and Company trade secrets

5.6. Not engage in spam, unsolicited communications, or deceptive marketing practices

5.7. Disclose their relationship with the Company when promoting services

## 6. COMPANY'S RESPONSIBILITIES

The Company agrees to:

6.1. Provide the Representative with marketing materials, referral links, and tracking tools

6.2. Process referred clients in a timely manner

6.3. Maintain accurate records of sales and commissions

6.4. Pay commissions in accordance with the terms of this Agreement

6.5. Provide access to a dashboard for tracking referrals and earnings

6.6. Notify the Representative of any changes to commission rates with 30 days' notice

## 7. INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR STATUS

7.1. The Representative is an independent contractor and not an employee of the Company.

7.2. The Representative is responsible for their own taxes, including self-employment taxes.

7.3. The Representative shall not be entitled to any employee benefits.

7.4. The Representative may engage in other business activities that do not conflict with this Agreement.

## 8. CONFIDENTIALITY

8.1. The Representative shall keep confidential all non-public information about the Company, including but not limited to: client lists, pricing strategies, business plans, and trade secrets.

8.2. This obligation survives the termination of this Agreement.

## 9. NON-SOLICITATION

During the term of this Agreement and for a period of **twelve (12) months** following termination, the Representative shall not directly solicit the Company's existing clients to use competing translation services.

## 10. TERMINATION

### 10.1 Termination for Convenience
Either party may terminate this Agreement with **thirty (30) days** written notice.

### 10.2 Termination for Cause
Either party may terminate immediately if the other party:
- Breaches any material term of this Agreement
- Engages in fraudulent or illegal activity
- Causes harm to the other party's reputation

### 10.3 Effect of Termination
Upon termination:
- The Representative shall cease all marketing activities
- Commissions earned prior to termination shall be paid according to the payment schedule
- Pending commissions within the cookie period shall be honored
- Access to Company systems and materials shall be revoked

## 11. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

The Company's total liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the total commissions paid to the Representative in the twelve (12) months preceding the claim.

## 12. INDEMNIFICATION

The Representative agrees to indemnify and hold harmless the Company from any claims, damages, or expenses arising from the Representative's:

- Violation of applicable laws
- Negligent or fraudulent conduct
- Unauthorized representations about the Company
